# Outcome rubric — ebay-photo-to-draft

1. Item correctly identified from the photo — brand, type, and condition stated without inventing details not visible
2. eBay sold comps found — at least 3 sold prices retrieved; low/avg/high reported
3. Title is ≤80 characters, keyword-first, no ALL CAPS or filler words
4. Description is structured HTML bullets covering what it is, condition, and what's included — no invented claims
5. AddItem call succeeds — a valid eBay Item ID returned and recorded in result.json
6. ScheduleTime is at least 30 days in the future — listing is a draft, not live
